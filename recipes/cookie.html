Description

These chewy chocolate chip cookies are the pinnacle of perfection! If you want a big, fat, soft, and chewy cookie like the kind you see at bakeries and specialty shops, then this is the recipe for you!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 325 degreesn F (165 degrees C). Grease cookie sheets or line with parchment paper.
  2. Sift flour, baking soda, and salt together; set aside.
  3. Beat brown sugar, melted butter, and white sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth.
  4. Beat in egg, egg yolk, and vanilla until light and creamy; add flour mixture and stir until dough is combined
  5. Stir in chocolate chips.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of dough 3 inches apart onto the prepared baking sheets.
  7. Bake cookies in the preheated oven until edges are golden about 15-17 minutes.
  8. Cool on the cookie sheets briefly before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  9. Enjoy!
